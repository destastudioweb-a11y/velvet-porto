Our Story

A family recipe, perfected

Velvet Bakery began in a small home kitchen with one recipe passed down three generations: a red velvet cake balanced between cocoa warmth and buttermilk tang. What started as weekend batches for neighbors is now a full pastry counter, though the process hasn't changed — everything is still mixed, layered, and iced by hand, every single morning.

We measure success one way: does it taste like it was made for you. That standard shapes every cake, cupcake, and cookie that leaves our ovens.
